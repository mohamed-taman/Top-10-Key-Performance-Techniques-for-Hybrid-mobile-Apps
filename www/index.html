Minimize Reflows

Minimizing Browser Reflows

  • Reduce the number of DOM elements
  • Minimize access to the DOM
  • Update elements "offline" before reinserting into DOM
  • Avoid tweaking layout in JavaScript
  • Batch your DOM changes, especially when updating styles.

Test on simulators vs. on real device

  • Simulators are just that: Simulators
  • Emulators are just that: Emulators
  • Simulators are fast, while emulators are slow.
  • Will never get the real feeling of your app touch responses, by using either simulators or emulators.
